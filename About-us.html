About TornaFarms Exports

Premium quality grapes for global markets. We specialize in exporting the finest grapes, delivering freshness and guaranteed quality to customers worldwide.

Our Story

Rooted in Tradition

GrapeVine Exports was established in 2015 with a passion for sharing the world's finest grapes with global markets. Founded by a family of grape growers with over three generations of experience, we understand the art and science of cultivating premium grapes.

Starting with a single vineyard partnership, we've grown to work with over 50 premium grape farms across different regions, each selected for their commitment to quality and sustainable farming practices.

Quality Above All

We believe that great grapes tell a story – from the soil they grow in to the care they receive throughout their journey. Our mission is to preserve the natural goodness and deliver grapes that meet the highest international standards.

Every grape that bears our name undergoes rigorous quality checks, ensuring that only the finest reach our customers. We're not just exporting fruit; we're sharing nature's bounty with the world.
