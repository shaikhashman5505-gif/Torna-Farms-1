About Torna Farms

With over two decades of agricultural excellence, Torna Farms stands as a trusted name in premium grape exports. Based in Nashik , we specialize in cultivating and delivering top-quality green and black grapes that meet global standards.

Our commitment to sustainable farming and modern post-harvest practices ensures freshness, safety, and unmatched taste. From Indian soil to European shelves, we promise quality, transparency, and satisfaction with every bunch.
